  • Bambi

    Appealing to the Young

    Bambi was a fun challenge that gave me a great opportunity to flex my UX/UI Designer muscles. Intended to be a service for young adults into the party nightlife, it had to facilitate meetups, and much of the content would be paid for by male members looking to invite groups of female members for a night out.

    To meet these challenges, I designed Wireframes, Branding, User Interface and was responsible for the User Experience. Additionally, I created all other elements of Graphic Design including Iconography, Banners and Responsive Email Marketing Templates.

    The end result is a modern, high-contrast and hip design that appeals to a younger crowd. The layout is both familiar and new, incorporating elements people expect with just a hint of something fresh.
