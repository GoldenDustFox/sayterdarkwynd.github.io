About Me:

With more than 12 years experience in Web Design and UX/UI Design, I've had the pleasure to work with many big-name clients on projects covering a wide range of areas developing industry-leading websites, apps and user experiences. My primary skills include HTML, CSS, LESS/SASS, UX/UI Design, Graphic Design, Storyboarding and Wireframing, while Sound and Video Editing, Spriting, Illustration, Inking, Colorist, Lua programming and JSON scripting round out my secondary skills.

I am adept with Adobe CS suite programs (Photoshop, Illustrator), have experience with Sketch, Excel, Omnigraffle, Powerpoint and most other tools of the trade. A fast learner, I can pick up new skills with minimal supervision.

A die-hard gamer, Tabletop roleplaying, videogames, board games...anything that engages the player in a fun and meaningful way calls to me. I also love taking things apart and turning them into something bigger...better. I've been tinkering with games since I was a kid and have made a second job out of modifying games into new experiences. My current project, FrackinUniverse, has 200,000 players worldwide!

I'm always seeking ways to improve both myself and the product to make the most out of every pixel. My philosophy is simple: Make design fun. If the user isn't engaged and interested...what's the point?

I bring many talents to the fore, and am willing and able to apply them in ways to truly make your project shine. If you're interested in reaching out to me for any sort of work you require, you can contact me below.
