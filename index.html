Hi, I'm Chris DeChamplain, a Web, Graphic and UX/UI Designer currently located in Toronto.

MY SPECIALTIES:

  • Responsive Web Design

    I've had extensive experience designing and building responsive websites and have developed an approach to my workflow that allows me to quickly tackle problems and offer viable solutions.

  • User Interfaces

    How people experience the web and apps matters, and I've had many opportunities to help reach the audience with friendly and intuitive interfaces. From app design to user-areas and beyond, I've got the chops to pull it off.

  • Worldbuilding

    Worldbuilding is essential to bringing digital universes to life, be it complicated rules systems or the setting itself. My work in designing rules sets, settings, cultures, scripts, level designs and many other facets in the gaming market are invaluable tools in shaping experiences with my audience.

  • Storytelling

    The brain hard-wired to enjoy stories, and bringing users engaging tales through design is among the best ways to evoke a response. Every tale can be shaped to reach an audience in engaging ways!

  • Concepts

    A jack-of-all-trades, I'm ever-curious and open-minded and always willing to learn. This has been instrumental in concepts and prototypes of digital work in my career.

  • Illustration

    A lot can be conveyed with a simple sketch, or a character design. I've done quite a bit of sketching, but my real forte is in colors. I've worked on comics, digital art, game art and more besides.

With more than 13 years experience in Web Design, Graphic Design and UX/UI Design, I've had the pleasure to work with clients on creative projects covering a wide range of areas developing industry-leading websites, apps and user experiences. My primary skills include Digital Marketing, HTML, CSS, UX/UI Design, Adobe CS, Wordpress, Wireframing, Concept/Prototyping and Storyboarding, while Sound and Video Editing, Spriting, Illustration, Inking, Colorist, Art Direction, Team Management, Lua programming and JSON scripting round out my secondary skills.

My philosophy is simple: Make design fun. If the user isn't engaged and interested...what's the point? The web is constantly changing, and we need to change with it. I aim to bring the best interaction and experience forward to thrill the user in order to keep them coming back.

Finally, I am a die-hard gamer with dreams of taking that industry by storm. My current mega-project (a mod for a game called Starbound), FrackinUniverse, has 200,000 players worldwide! There I've led a team in creating art assets, level designs, scripting, writing, programming and more over the last several years. As a result, it has grown into one of the largest and most comprehensive overhauls ever released for a game!
